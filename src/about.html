About Mineocity

Mineocity Hosting was started in 2011 as a brand of Minecraft Server Hosting that to put it simply was intended to be simple.

Since 2011 we have grown at an astonishing rate that has allowed us to create partnerships to benefit the customers who realize how great we are at what we do.

We know what its like to run servers when the server you rent lag. We know that lag takes the fun out of gaming so we have built our system to remove all types of lag that we can.

Careers

Our managment team is dirven to help our team members suceed and we have supervisors available to help guide you to fix any issue that you cannot find a solution to. Every member of our managment team started as an entry level tech support agent and has since moved through the ranks to where they are now.

We are a company that focuses on the customer and as such we do everything in our power to help them and their server suceed. Therfore we are seeking energetic applicants who want to help us make a difference and have fun at the same time.

Tech Support

We are currently looking for Tech Support staff. Being a member of Mineocity Hosting's support staff will allow you to join a team of enthusiastic and experienced sales and tech support agents. Most of our staff members have been working with us for over a year and are very satisifed with what they do for us.

If you have experience managing and troubleshooting Minecraft, Tekkit, FTB, & VPS server then you may be just the person we are looking for.

Requirements:

  • Friendly, helpful, and optimistic attitude
  • Must be familiar with WHMCS, Multicraft, Minecraft, and basic understanding of FTB & Tekkit mod packs
  • Must be fluent in English and able to read, write, and speak English without use of translators

Pay is determined on a case-by-case basis but is all contract based flat rate pay. We do not offer hourly or salary based pay for this position. All applicants must be over the age of sixteen years old as we must comply with labor laws of New York State.
